Major Subdivision

Review Process

  1. Optional Conceptual Review with staff
  2. Preliminary Plat Submitted[1]
  3. Staff field inspections
  4. Technical Review Committee meeting
  5. Comments/Corrections sent to Engineer/Surveyor
  6. Submit revised plats
  7. Staff reports available
  8. Planning Commission Workshop
  9. Planning Commission Meeting
    1. denial - return to Step 2
    2. postponement
    3. approval (w/conditions) - continue to Step 10
  10. File Construction Plans[2] for Planning Commission Engineer review and City Engineer (if applicable)
  11. Installation of erosion control measures
  12. Begin construction and grading[3]
  13. File bond/letter of credit for final course of asphalt and drainage (and landscaping, if required)
  14. File Final Plat[4] (1 mylar and 8 prints)
  15. Staff review and certification for recording
  16. File record plat with Scott County Clerk's Office
  17. Return 1 mylar and 8 paper copies to Planning Commission office; 1 paper to be distributed by Planning Commission to Building Office

NOTE: Amendments to the Final Subdivision Plat will be reviewed on an individual basis. Based on the degree of amendment/revision, a revised Preliminary Subdivision Plat may be required for review and approval by the full Planning Commission (must be submitted by the application deadline). Final Subdivision Plats that require full Planning Commission review must be submitted by the application deadline.
